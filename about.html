About

Coherence Field Theory is a structural science focused on how coherence forms, stabilizes, and collapses across systems. It examines the architectural forces that govern alignment, drift, and field compatibility, independent of narrative or domain.

The discipline isolates the conditions under which systems maintain structural integrity or lose it through drift, offering a framework for understanding behavior at the architectural layer.
