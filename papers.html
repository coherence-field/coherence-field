Paper 2: Drift Mechanics

Abstract: Drift is the dynamic expression of incoherence. This paper formalizes drift as a mechanical process with identifiable stages, thresholds, and propagation patterns. It examines how distortion accumulates, how compensatory strategies propagate misalignment, and how drift thresholds determine stabilization, collapse, or recovery. Drift mechanics provide a diagnostic and predictive framework for understanding system behavior under varying field conditions.
